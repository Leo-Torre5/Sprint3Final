Library Policies

Our Wishes

To make sure your musical journey with us is as delightful as the melodies we offer, we've crafted some friendly guidelines. By borrowing from our library, you're not just accessing records; you're becoming a cherished part of the Melody Matrix community. Here's what you need to know:

Borrower Eligibility

To keep our musical haven close-knit, we ask that borrowers be residents of Omaha, Nebraska. This ensures that our collection resonates with the local community. Your valid proof of residency is like a backstage pass, inviting you into our harmonious world.

Library Card

Borrowing records is a breeze with your Melody Matrix Library Card. Don't worry; it's on the house! This card is your golden ticket to our collection, making your journey through musical genres a smooth process.

Borrowing Limits

We want you to explore, but to make sure everyone gets a chance, each borrower can check out up to five records at a time. It's like having your own mini-concert at home! And with a two-week loan period, there's ample time to savor the musical magic.

Respect Our Vinyls

Vinyl records are like old friends, handle them with care. Keep them snug in their protective sleeves when not in use, ensuring that the soulful tunes remain crisp and clear.

Handling and Storage

Proper handling is a key to preserving the magic. Hold records by the edges, avoiding the grooves to keep the vibes undisturbed. Store them in a cool, dry place away from harsh elementsâ€”your records will thank you with timeless melodies.

Late Returns

Return your records by the due date, and the music will keep flowing smoothly. Late returns? No worries, but a small fine of $1 per day for each overdue item ensures fairness for all.

Damaged or Lost Records

Report any damage or loss immediately. While accidents happen, a little responsibility goes a long way. Replacement or repair costs may apply, ensuring the ongoing harmony of our collection.

Renewals

Renew it for another two-week serenade, as long as no one else is waiting in line. It's like adding an encore to your musical experience.

Communication

We're all ears for your thoughts. Share your feedback and suggestions to help us fine-tune our musical services. Your input is the melody that shapes a better experience for everyone.

Compliance with Policies

By borrowing from Melody Matrix, you're joining a musical pact. Following these guidelines ensures that our musical symphony stays in tune. Straying from the notes? That might lead to a brief pause in library privileges.

Policy Updates

Our lending policy is like a living score, evolving to enhance your musical journey. Check in periodically for updates, so you're always on beat with the latest harmonies.

Thank you for being a part of the Melody Matrix community. We hope this lending policy enhances your musical journey and fosters a love for vinyl records. If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to reach out to our support team. Enjoy the music!
