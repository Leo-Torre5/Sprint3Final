About The Music Library

Our Story

In the heart of Omaha, a unique music library, Melody Matrix, was born in the 1990s, thanks to the vision of Mr. Jason Cooper, a dedicated music collector with an ardent desire to share the gift of music. Mr. Cooper's personal vinyl record collection served as the foundation for this remarkable endeavor, with the sole purpose of lending his cherished records to the people of Omaha, much like a library lends books.

Initially, the library was a cozy nook that hosted Mr. Cooper's vinyl treasures. As word spread about this unique idea, residents started to embrace the idea of sharing their own beloved records with the community. Inspired by the harmony and camaraderie that vinyl records brought, they began donating their vinyl to Melody Matrix. It didn't take long for the library's collection to grow, and before anyone knew it, it had surpassed 2000 vinyl records, each with its own story and melody.

Today, that vision lives on through the passionate dedication of Emma Cooper, the torchbearer of her father's dream. She believes that music is not only about what you hear but also about the memories, the emotions, and the connection it evokes. With her love for music and a desire to bridge the past and the present, she has embarked on an ambitious journey to create this digital platform that respects the tradition of Melody Matrix while embracing the convenience of the modern age.

Our Mission

Our website is the embodiment of that vision. It is a testament to our commitment to preserving the spirit of sharing and community that Melody Matrix was founded on. As we enter the digital age, we are excited to offer you an opportunity to explore our extensive vinyl catalog from the comfort of your own home.

Discover a treasure trove of over 2000 vinyl records, each with a unique story and melody. From timeless classics to hidden gems, our collection represents the tapestry of musical history. Dive into the world of music as you read detailed information about each record. Learn about the artist, the album, and the stories behind the melodies that have moved generations. Like a traditional library, we still believe in sharing. With just a few clicks, you can request loans, ensuring that the spirit of community and the love of music continue to thrive.

At Melody Matrix, we are not just a digital platform; we are a bridge between the past and the present. We honor the legacy of Mr. Jason Cooper and celebrate the dedication of his daughter, Emma, who believes that music is a timeless gift meant to be shared. Our website is not just a place to explore vinyl records; it's a place to immerse yourself in the stories, emotions, and melodies that define our history.

Join us on this journey as we keep the melody alive, one click at a time.
