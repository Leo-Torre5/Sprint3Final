Privacy Policy

Our Promise

At Melody Matrix, we take the preservation of your musical experience seriously, just as we value your privacy and security. Our commitment extends to creating a harmonious world of music where you can explore, enjoy, and share your passion for music without any compromise. Here's how we handle your information in our harmonious world of music.

Information We Collect

To ensure the smooth rhythm of your borrowing experience, we collect essential details, including your name, address, and email. These details are used to create your personalized online library card, granting you access to our extensive vinyl collection.

How We Use Your Information

Your data forms the cornerstone of our service. We use it to facilitate the borrowing process, ensuring timely access to our vinyl collection and personalized experiences tailored to your musical preferences.

Data Security

Our commitment to safeguarding your information is unwavering. We employ industry-standard security measures to ensure that your data remains as secure as a treasured vinyl record.

Your Rights, Your Melody

You hold the conductor's baton when it comes to your data. You have the right to access, modify, or even delete your data. Feel free to contact us to exercise these rights or request more information about your musical journey.

Policy Updates, Keeping the Beat

Just like a great melody evolves, our practices and policies may change. We may update this policy to reflect the changes in our symphony or in response to regulations. So, be sure to check back periodically for any harmonious updates.

Thank you for choosing Melody Matrix, your trust and satisfaction compose the melody that fuels our passion for sharing music!
